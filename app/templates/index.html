What is Spaced Repetition Scheduler?

Spaced Repetition Scheduler is a free web application that automatically creates study review schedules in your Google Calendar. When you sign in with your Google account and enter a topic you're learning (like "Python Programming" or "Organic Chemistry"), our app creates calendar events on scientifically-proven intervals (default: 1, 3, 7, and 21 days later) to help you review and retain information long-term. We only create new calendar events - we never read, modify, or delete your existing calendar data. This app is completely free and built to help students and lifelong learners master any subject.
