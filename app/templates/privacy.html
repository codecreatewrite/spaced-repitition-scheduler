Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect & Google Calendar Access

When you use StudyCore, we collect:

  • Google Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account
  • Calendar Data: We create study review events in your Google Calendar based on your input
  • Usage Data: Topics you create schedules for and your review intervals

We request access to your Google Calendar solely to:

  • Create study review events in your calendar

We do NOT:

  • Read your existing calendar events
  • Edit or delete your existing events
  • Access calendars you haven't selected
  • Share your calendar data with anyone
  • Use your calendar data for any purpose other than creating review schedules

Scope we request: calendar.events (create and manage events only)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity via Google Sign-In
  • Create StudyCore review events in your Google Calendar
  • Display your schedules in your dashboard

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and temporarily. We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Read your existing calendar events
  • Access any Google data beyond what you explicitly authorize

4. Google API Services

This app's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke access at any time via your Google Account permissions
  • Delete calendar events we created at any time
  • Request deletion of your account data by contacting us
